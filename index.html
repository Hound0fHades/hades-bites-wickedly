Lore of the Underworld Kitchen

About the Hound of Hades

A food truck born from flame, shadow, and a long-standing dream — built for those who crave something a little darker, a little witchier, and a lot more flavorful.

The Infernal Founder

Hades Bites Wickedly is helmed by Heather, known in the realm of the brand as The Hound of Hades — guardian of flavor, keeper of the ember flame, and alchemist of wicked comfort food.

With a love for witchy aesthetics, fantasy worlds, and soul-warming food, the Underworld Kitchen balances dark, immersive storytelling with real-world accessibility: kid-friendly options, allergy-aware offerings, and flexible heat levels for every mortal.

“Everyone deserves a little magic with their meal — whether they walk in darkness or in daylight.”
What the Underworld Stands For

✶ All are welcome at the cauldron. From picky eaters and cautious kids to spice fiends and goth foodies — there is a path through the Underworld for you.

✶ Allergy-aware by design. Key items can be made gluten-friendly, dairy-free, or vegan-friendly, with clear communication at the window.

✶ Story-driven, heart-led food. Every dish, name, and visual is part of a living mythology built to be fun, immersive, and unforgettable.
